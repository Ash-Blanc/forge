You are helping to brainstorm practical product and venture ideas that a solo developer could realistically prototype.
Constraints:


Assume only moderate front‑end and JavaScript skills; avoid suggesting ideas that require complex, highly interactive UIs or large engineering teams.


Tasks:


Propose 7–10 concrete product ideas that solve real problems for clearly identifiable users.


For each idea, briefly describe: who it is for, the core value proposition, and the typical user workflow.


Highlight the 2–3 ideas that seem especially strong as lean MVPs and explain why (feasibility, clarity of value, and ease of getting to a first version).
